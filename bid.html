From RFQ to Work Order.

1

A buyer posts a requirement

Material, quantity, and delivery location are submitted as a verified RFQ on Build360. High-value contracts default to sealed-bid mode : all bids hidden until the deadline.

2

The RFQ goes live (Ranked Model)

Published to the vendor dashboard with a live timer. You accept the RFQ to unlock bidding. You see your rank only : not competitor prices.

3

Submit & improve your rank

Enter your lowest all-inclusive price. The system ranks you among all participants. If you're #3, bid lower to reach #1. Aggressive pricing wins.

4

Build360 awards the RFQ

When the countdown hits zero, the lowest bid wins. For sealed-bid RFQs, all bids are revealed and the winner is announced simultaneously.
