// Vendor Bidding Platform

Approved RFQs.
Lowest Price Wins.

Verified buyer requirements flow into this dashboard. Registered supply partners compete by submitting their lowest all-inclusive supply price — Build360 awards the RFQ to the most competitive bid before the timer ends.

From RFQ to Work Order.

1

A buyer posts a requirement

Material, quantity, and delivery location are submitted as a verified RFQ on Build360.

2

The RFQ goes live

Once verified, the requirement is published to this vendor dashboard with a delivery-linked bidding window.

3

Vendors register & log in

Verified supply partners sign up once, then log in to view all currently open RFQs.

4

Accept & submit your lowest price

Accept the RFQ to participate, then enter the lowest all-inclusive supply price you can operate at to win the bid.

5

Build360 awards the RFQ

When the countdown hits zero, Build360 reviews all bids and awards the work order to the vendor with the most competitive (lowest) price.
